April Fools' Mashed Potatoes

Nothing beats Mashed Potatoes, but what about a dessert that looks like mashed potatoes?! This is our favorite easy April Fools Prank Food!

Ingredients

  • Vanilla cheesecake
  • Caramel syrup
  • Sea salt flakes
  • Dark chocolate

Directions

  1. Remove the crust if needed and add the cheesecake to a plate.
  2. Mash down the cheesecake with a fork and/or spoon to resemble mashed potatoes.
  3. Right before serving, drizzle on caramel syrup and sprinkle with sea salt flakes.
  4. Use the fine holes of a cheese grater to grate a piece of dark chocolate to look like pepper.
